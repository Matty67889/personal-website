Crack Door

You give the deal a powerful blow to the crack, and it immediately crumbles before your might. To your surprise, you aren't immediately greeted by the dark light and bitter cold of the night. The crumbling of the crack revealed a small passageway, issuing a bright light at its end.

You decide to crawl down the path to see where it will lead you. After about a minute of crawling, you are met with a small arched door that you are sure you can fit through. You try to open it, but it seems to be locked.

"Damnit, now I need a key?" you think angrily. It feels like I'm so close but these silly obstacles keep getting in my way.

What will you do now?

All of a sudden, you remember that you have the key that you got earlier in your pocket. Awesome! Now you can make it through the door and see what awaits you!
