Dining Room

You come across a room an extremely dusty room with 6 chairs surrounding a table. From this alone, you detemine that this room used to be a dining room. It reminds you of Alice in Wonderland, specifically the scene where Alice had the tea party with the Mad Hatter and the other creatures of her imagination. Except now the scene wasn't as colorful; there were no guests, no tea, no food, and rubble covering the table.

On the side of the table appeared to be a small cupboard, with a single in-tact jar sitting solemnly on its counter. Some drawers of the cupboard were opened, and their contents taken, as if a very hungry someone had been rumaging through it looking for a snack.

Across the room from the cupboard, on the other side of the table, there is another, larger cupboard, who has a much larger storage capability. It has also been deprived of its contents and also has a single storage vessel on its counter, a teapot.

"This person really likes their teapots I guess," you muse.

On one side of the room, you notice a window with bright light emitting through it. You find it odd, because you and your friends arrived at the haunted house at an extremely late time, so it sould not be sunny outside. You conclude that either time is warped in this house, or your eyes are playing tricks on you.

You consider if you should inspect the window to find out what's going on, or if you should just keep exploring rooms.

You walk up to the window and try to look up out of it, but see nothing but the bright light striking your face. You try opening the window, but it does not budge. You look closer at the window, and realize that the light coming from it is only a painting that is somehow emitting light. Mysterious forces are at work in this house.
